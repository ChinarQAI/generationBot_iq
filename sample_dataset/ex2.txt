Request for Proposal

RFP: Website Redesign and Development
Proposal Due By: May 15, 2024
ABC Corporation

Project Overview:

ABC Corporation is seeking proposals from qualified web development agencies to redesign and develop its corporate website. The primary objective of this project is to enhance the user experience, improve website functionality, and modernize the design to better reflect our brand identity.

Project Goals:

Create a visually appealing and intuitive website layout that effectively communicates our company's values, products, and services.
Implement responsive design elements to ensure optimal viewing experience across all devices and screen sizes.
Enhance website performance, including faster loading times and improved navigation for visitors.
Scope of Work:

The selected agency will be responsible for:

Collaborating with ABC Corporation to define project requirements and design preferences.
Developing wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for review and feedback.
Building a fully functional website using the latest web development technologies and best practices.
Integrating SEO strategies to improve search engine visibility and organic traffic.
Providing training and documentation to enable ABC Corporation's staff to manage and update the website content independently.
Current Roadblocks and Barriers to Success:

Outdated website design and layout that does not effectively engage visitors or showcase our offerings.
Limited mobile responsiveness, resulting in a subpar user experience for smartphone and tablet users.
Inefficient content management system (CMS) hindering the ability to update and maintain the website easily.
Evaluation Metrics and Criteria:

Previous experience and portfolio demonstrating successful website redesign projects.
Expertise in user experience (UX) design principles and responsive web development techniques.
Proposed approach and timeline for the project, including milestones and deliverables.
Cost-effectiveness and adherence to the specified budget.
Client references and testimonials from past projects.
Submission Requirements:

Interested agencies must submit the following:

Detailed proposal outlining their approach to website redesign and development.
Portfolio showcasing relevant work examples and case studies.
Proposed timeline, including key milestones and project phases.
Estimated budget breakdown, including itemized costs for design, development, and ongoing support.
Contact information for key personnel involved in the project.
